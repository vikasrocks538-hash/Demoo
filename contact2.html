Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I book an appointment?

You can call us directly at 1234567890, send a WhatsApp message, or use the online registration form on our homepage — all responses are confirmed within 24 hours.

What are your clinic hours?

We are open Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 7 PM, and Saturday 9 AM – 5 PM. We are closed on Sundays but available for urgent enquiries by phone.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please carry a valid photo ID, any previous medical records or prescriptions, and your insurance card if applicable. New patients may come 10 mins early.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash, UPI, debit/credit cards and most major health insurance plans. Please call ahead to confirm insurance coverage.

Do you handle emergency cases?

For medical emergencies, please call us immediately. We will advise whether to visit the clinic directly or direct you to the nearest emergency facility.

How do follow-up appointments work?

Follow-up visits can be booked via call or WhatsApp. We maintain records of all treatments so continuity of care is seamless and efficient.
